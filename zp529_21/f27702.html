These Wigwam(R) Ultra Cool-Lite Low socks offer an exceptionally smooth and comfortable feel with performance engineering to let you really take on any challenge!
Low-cut socks that sit at the ankle.
Sold as one-pair pack.
Ultra-lightweight socks have cushioning in critical zones and are designed to have an invisible feel.
X20(R) advanced moisture-absorbing acrylic fiber is engineered to have capillary capacity to absorb moisture, and then help it evaporate.
Ultimax(R) features moisture repelling fibers and moisture transfer fibers in the instep and leg to move moisture from the foot up to the leg so that it can evaporate more easily and help prevent blistering.
Cushioning on the heel and toe help provide comfort with minimal bulk.
Breathable instep helps keep your foot dry.
Seamless toe closure helps reduce chafing.
41% nylon, 30% polyester, 19% X2O(R) Acrylic, 6% spandex, 4% Tencel(R).
